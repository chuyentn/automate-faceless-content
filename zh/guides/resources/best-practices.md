# ✅ Best Practices

Essential best practices for faceless video content creation success.

---

## Content Creation

- **Post consistently** — aim for 1-3 videos per day minimum
- **Batch create** — make a week's worth of content in one session
- **Hook viewers in 3 seconds** — your opening line determines everything
- **Keep videos focused** — one topic per video

## Platform Optimization

- **YouTube**: Use keywords in titles, descriptions, and tags
- **TikTok**: Use trending sounds and hashtags
- **Instagram**: Post Reels at peak engagement hours (7-9 AM, 12-2 PM)
- **Facebook**: Longer videos (3-5 min) tend to perform better

## Monetization

- **Diversify income streams** — don't rely on AdSense alone
- **Add affiliate links** to every video description
- **Build an email list** from day one
- **Create digital products** once you have an audience
