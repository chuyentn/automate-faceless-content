# 💳 Credit Management

How to manage and optimize your credits on [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=victorchuyen68).

---

## Understanding Credits

- Each video creation uses a certain number of credits
- Credits refresh monthly based on your subscription plan
- Higher plans include more credits and advanced features

## Tips to Maximize Credits

1. **Plan your content first** — don't waste credits on test videos
2. **Use Bulk Scheduler** — batch create for efficiency
3. **Repurpose content** — turn 1 long video into multiple shorts
4. **Use Idea Discovery first** — research before creating
