# 🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

Common issues and solutions for faceless video content creation.

---

## Video Quality Issues

### Problem: Video looks blurry or low quality
- **Solution**: Make sure you're exporting at 1080p minimum
- Use the highest quality settings in [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=victorchuyen68)

### Problem: AI-generated voice sounds robotic
- **Solution**: Try different voice options in the video generator
- Adjust speed and tone settings for more natural results

---

## Account & Platform Issues

### Problem: Videos not uploading to social media
- **Solution**: Reconnect your social media accounts
- Check that your accounts have posting permissions enabled

### Problem: YouTube not showing monetization option
- **Solution**: Ensure you meet the minimum requirements (500 subscribers + 3,000 watch hours)
- See our [Getting Monetized Fast Guide](../monetization/getting-monetized-fast.md)

---

## Content Strategy Issues

### Problem: Low views on videos
- **Solution**: Focus on thumbnails and titles — they determine 80% of clicks
- Use the [Idea Discovery](../features/idea-discovery.md) tool for trending topics

### Problem: Not sure what niche to choose
- **Solution**: See our [Niche Selection Guide](../niches/niche-selection.md)
- Start with what you know, then expand
