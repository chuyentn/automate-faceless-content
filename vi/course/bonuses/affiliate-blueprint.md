# 💰 Affiliate Marketing Blueprint

A step-by-step blueprint for earning through affiliate marketing with faceless video content.

---

## The Affiliate Marketing Model

1. **Choose products** related to your niche
2. **Create review/tutorial videos** using [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=victorchuyen68)
3. **Add affiliate links** in video descriptions
4. **Scale with bulk content** — more videos = more opportunities

## Recommended Affiliate Programs

| Program | Commission | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Amazon Associates | 1-10% | Product reviews |
| Syllaby.io Affiliate | Recurring | Content creators |
| ClickBank | 50-75% | Digital products |
| ShareASale | Varies | Multiple niches |
| Impact | Varies | SaaS & tech |

## Best Practices

- **Disclose** affiliate relationships (required by FTC)
- **Only promote** products you believe in
- **Create comparison videos** — they convert best
- **Pin affiliate links** in comments
- **Use URL shorteners** for cleaner links
